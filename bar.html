WORK ABOUT

The Bar

Overview

The bar is a virtual dating experience that aims to simulate the hooking up/dating scene in real life and innovate the behavior of interaction.

Role

Avatar Design, Modeling, Rigging, Scene Design

Tools

Unity, Blender

Goals

  • - Recreate the dating scene that was shut down because of the pandemic
  • - Create a virtual space for people to connect, and develop relationship
  • - Explore the usage of controller as part of multiplayer interaction
  • - Broaden the horizon on conveying intimacy in VR
  • - Construct a safe space for VR user to communicate

Features

Flirt
  • - Users can send a hint to others, so as to show their interest to other users.
  • - The visual effect will be only seen between two people.
  • - The receiver will also get haptic feedback.
Buy a drink
  • - User can spend their token and buy other a drink.
  • - The receiver can decide whether he or she wants to receive it or return it.
Dance Floor
  • - Users can dance with others in this venue!
  • - The “live” DJ will play the music all night in VR.
Social Bubble
  • - Users can launch a social bubble to create a private space.
  • - When Social Bubble is up, users can block others from hearing their talking.
  • - Also, the volume of background music will be lower.
