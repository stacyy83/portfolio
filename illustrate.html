MEDITATION

I was inspired by the lotus first. The lotus is a symbol of Buddha and also possesses the meaning of beauty. Therefore, I drew a girl whose fingers were in the shape of a Buddha's hand and with a mysterious vibe.

MIND FOREST

I was inspired by one of my favorite artists Fumi Mini Nakamura; I attempted to utilize some flowers, animals and hands to design this project as she usually did. I wanted to express the pure part in our deep mind although we live in the complicated world.

SAVE US

I want to express the global warming problem that affects the environment and wild animals. The melting ice is a symbol of global warming because of the high temperatures on Earth. I also placed the lifebuoy and “save me” words with a broken iceberg to show the irreversible situation that wild animals are encountering.
