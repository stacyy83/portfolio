Life of Tangerine is a critical object that shows the food waste problem in the world.

Framework

Topic: Food waste

Attribute: Interactive

Device: Symbolism

Mood: Surprise

Tools

Arduino

Motivation

Since I care about the environment, I want to focus the topic on food waste problem. I did some research about food waste and found this article said that 1/3 of all food produced globally goes to waste, which is really surprised me. Also, the video called Life of A Strawberry on Youtube makes me want to show the process of the waste. We put a lot of resources and effort into producing food but ultimately throw into the trash without eating. It provides me a strong incentive to make a critical object and throw the light on this problem.
